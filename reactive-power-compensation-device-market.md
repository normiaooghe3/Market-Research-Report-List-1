<p><p>The Reactive Power Compensation Device market consists of two primary types: Static Var Compensators (SVC) and Static Var Generators (SVG). SVCs utilize thyristors to control reactive power by adjusting the voltage levels in power systems, improving stability and efficiency. In contrast, SVGs employ advanced power electronics to provide faster and more reliable reactive power compensation, enhancing overall performance. Both technologies aim to mitigate voltage fluctuations, improve power factor, and support grid stability in various industrial and utility applications.</p></p>

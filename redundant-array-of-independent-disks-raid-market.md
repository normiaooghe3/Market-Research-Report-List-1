<p><p>Redundant Array of Independent Disks (RAID) enhances data storage reliability and performance by combining multiple disks into a single unit. The market for RAID is segmented into hardware and software RAID solutions. Hardware RAID employs dedicated controller cards that manage disk operations independently of the system’s CPU, providing higher performance and advanced features. In contrast, software RAID relies on the operating system to manage disks, offering flexibility and cost-effectiveness, though often with less performance compared to hardware solutions.</p></p>

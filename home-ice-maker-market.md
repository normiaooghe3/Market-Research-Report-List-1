<p><ul><li>Water-Cooled Ice Machine</li><li>Air-Cooled Ice Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home ice maker market features two main types: water-cooled and air-cooled ice machines. Water-cooled ice machines use a water supply to cool the condenser, offering efficient cooling, particularly in high-capacity settings but requiring a constant water source. In contrast, air-cooled ice machines rely on ambient air for cooling, making them more suitable for residential use due to easier installation and lower water usage. Each type caters to different needs based on efficiency, installation requirements, and operational costs.</p></p>

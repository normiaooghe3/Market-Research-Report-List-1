<p><p>The construction tractors market is primarily divided into two types: crawler tractors and wheeled tractors. Crawler tractors, equipped with continuous tracks, excel in rough terrains, providing stability and superior traction for heavy-duty tasks. They are ideal for excavation and grading. Wheeled tractors, on the other hand, are designed for higher speeds and maneuverability on smoother surfaces, making them suitable for urban construction and roadwork. Both types cater to various construction needs, enhancing efficiency in different environments.</p></p>

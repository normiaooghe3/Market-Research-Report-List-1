<p><p>The garden tractors market is categorized into wheeled and crawler tractors. Wheeled tractors are versatile, offering ease of movement across various terrains, making them ideal for tasks like mowing and tilling. They come in various horsepower ratings and are popular among homeowners and small farms. Crawler tractors, on the other hand, feature tracks instead of wheels, providing superior traction and stability on uneven or muddy surfaces. They are better suited for heavy-duty tasks like land clearing and digging, appealing to more intensive agricultural applications.</p></p>

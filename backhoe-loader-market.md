<p><ul><li>Articulated Backhoe Loader</li><li>Rigidity Backhoe Loader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The backhoe loader market comprises two primary types: articulated and rigid backhoe loaders. Articulated backhoe loaders feature a flexible joint between the front and rear sections, allowing for improved maneuverability and ease of navigation in tight spaces, making them ideal for urban construction. In contrast, rigid backhoe loaders have a fixed connection, providing enhanced stability and power for heavy-duty tasks. Both types are essential for excavating, digging, and material handling in various construction and landscaping applications.</p></p>

<p><p>Strainers are essential in filtration systems, categorized into several types. Y type strainers, shaped like a "Y," efficiently filter liquids and are easy to clean. Basket strainers feature a cylindrical mesh basket that captures debris, ideal for larger flow rates. Duplex strainers consist of two chambers, allowing for continuous operation during maintenance.

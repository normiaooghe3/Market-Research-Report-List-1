<p><ul><li>Extrusion</li><li>Powder Bonding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D construction printers market encompasses various technologies, primarily categorized into extrusion and powder bonding methods. Extrusion-based printers use materials like concrete or thermoplastics, layering them to create structures with precision and speed. In contrast, powder bonding techniques involve applying a binding agent to powdered materials, which are then fused to form solid structures. Both methods enable innovative construction processes, reducing labor costs and environmental impact while allowing for complex designs and rapid prototyping in the building industry.</p></p>

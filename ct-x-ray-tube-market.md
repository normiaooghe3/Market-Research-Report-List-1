<p><strong>The CT X-ray Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Anode</li><li>Rotating Anode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CT X-ray tube market is primarily divided into two types: stationary anode and rotating anode tubes. Stationary anode tubes feature a fixed anode that offers simplicity and lower costs, making them suitable for basic imaging applications. In contrast, rotating anode tubes have an anode that spins during operation, allowing for higher heat dissipation and improved image quality. This capability makes rotating anode tubes ideal for advanced imaging needs, contributing to their growing adoption in clinical settings.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Jacketed Vessels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Jackets</li><li>Half-Pipe Coil Jackets</li><li>Dimple Jackets</li><li>Plate Coils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The jacketed vessels market features various types designed for efficient thermal management. Conventional jackets provide uniform heating or cooling around the vessel, while half-pipe coil jackets enhance heat exchange with a series of pipes welded to the vessel's exterior. Dimple jackets utilize a series of dimples to create a larger surface area for heat transfer, improving efficiency. Plate coils consist of flat plates formed into coils, offering flexibility and effective thermal control. Each design caters to specific operational needs and process requirements.</p></p>

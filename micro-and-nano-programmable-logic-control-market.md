<p><p>The micro and nano programmable logic control (PLC) market is primarily divided into fixed and modular PLC types. Fixed PLCs are compact, all-in-one units designed for specific applications, offering limited expandability but ease of installation and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, modular PLCs provide flexibility and scalability, allowing users to customize configurations by adding or removing modules based on varying operational needs. This adaptability makes modular PLCs suitable for complex automation processes that may require frequent adjustments or expansions.</p></p>

<p><p>The Aluminum Oil Cooler market is segmented into Single Pass and Dual Pass types, each offering distinct designs for efficient cooling. Single Pass coolers circulate oil through a single flow path, providing effective heat exchange with lower complexity and cost. In contrast, Dual Pass coolers allow oil to flow through two paths, enhancing heat transfer efficiency and cooling performance, making them suitable for high-demand applications. Both types are widely used in automotive and industrial sectors, catering to varied cooling needs.</p></p>
